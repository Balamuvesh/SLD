Generalized Anxiety Disorder Test

Uses an inset box shadow for the bordered effect on the state.


1. When you are all at home for the day, does your child follow you around the house wherever you go?


2. Does your child often come home sick from school with a stomach ache or headache?


3. Is your child easily fatigued and/or complain of sore muscles?


4. Has your child ever gotten so scared that she/he says it’s hard to breathe?


5. At birthday parties, does your child feel nervous if left with people he/she doesn’t know very well?


6. Are you woken frequently in the night because your child had another nightmare about something bad happening to you?


7. Does your child change clothes several times a day because they sweats a lot when they are nervous?


8. Is your child scared to get on the bus and go to school every day?


9. Does your child expect the worst, even when there is no justification for worry?


10. Even when you are upstairs and your child is in the basement, does she/he get scared about being alone in the house?


11. Does your child fill her bed with stuffed animals and the family pet because she/he is too nervous to sleep alone?


12. Has your child come home from a slumber party because he/she’s too scared to sleep away from home?


13. Have teachers mentioned that your child seems nervous working in groups or speaking in class?


14. After school, does your child spend an excessive amount of time doing and redoing homework worksheets because he doesn’t want anything to be less than perfect?


15. Is your child irrationally afraid of flying in an airplane, or of a certain animal?


16. Does your child tremble when she’s in crowded places like the shopping mall or a busy playground?


17. Does your child require constant approval and reassurance that he/she has done a good job?
